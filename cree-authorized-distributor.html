Authorization FAQ

How can I verify you are an authorized distributor?

You can verify our authorization by contacting Wolfspeed directly at their official website, or by requesting our authorization certificate. We are also listed in Wolfspeed's partner directory.

What regions do you cover as an authorized distributor?

We are authorized to distribute Cree and Wolfspeed products globally, with local support and warehousing in North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific regions.

Do you provide certificates of authenticity?

Yes, we provide a certificate of authenticity with every order. This certificate includes lot numbers, date codes, and confirms the products are genuine Cree/Wolfspeed products.

What is the difference between authorized and unauthorized distributors?

Authorized distributors have direct relationships with manufacturers, provide genuine products with full warranties, and offer technical support. Unauthorized (gray market) sources may sell counterfeit, damaged, or outdated products without warranty protection.

Can I get technical support for products purchased from you?

Yes, as an authorized distributor, we provide full technical support including product selection guidance, application engineering assistance, and direct escalation to Wolfspeed engineers when needed.
