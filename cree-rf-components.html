About Cree RF Solutions

Cree (Wolfspeed) RF components leverage the superior properties of Gallium Nitride (GaN) on Silicon Carbide (SiC) substrate technology. These devices deliver unmatched power density, efficiency, and bandwidth for demanding RF applications.

GaN on SiC Advantages

  • Highest power density in the industry
  • Wide bandwidth operation (DC to 100+ GHz)
  • High breakdown voltage and thermal conductivity
  • Excellent linearity for communications
  • Ruggedness under mismatch conditions

MMIC Amplifiers

Integrated Solutions

Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit (MMIC) amplifiers provide complete RF amplification solutions in compact packages. Ideal for systems requiring high integration and consistent performance.

Product Types

  • Low Noise Amplifiers (LNA): High sensitivity receiver front-ends
  • Power Amplifiers (PA): High power transmitter output stages
  • Driver Amplifiers: Intermediate gain stages
  • Wideband Amplifiers: Multi-octave coverage
