XHP Series

Extreme High Power

The XHP (Extreme High Power) series delivers maximum lumen density with multi-die technology. Available in 35, 50, and 70 configurations for different output requirements.

  • XHP35: Up to 1800 lumens
  • XHP50: Up to 2500 lumens
  • XHP70: Up to 4000+ lumens
  • Package: 6.5mm x 6.5mm (XHP50/70)
  • High lumen density

Applications

  • High-output area lighting
  • Sports lighting
  • Searchlights
  • High-performance flashlights
  • Automotive forward lighting
