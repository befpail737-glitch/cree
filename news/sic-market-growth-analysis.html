SiC Component Market to Grow 28% Annually Through 2028

November 30, 2023 By LiTong Group

Market analysis indicates that the Silicon Carbide (SiC) component market will experience strong growth, expanding at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 28% through 2028.

Market Drivers

Several factors are driving this growth:

  • Rapid adoption of electric vehicles (EVs)
  • Growth in renewable energy installations
  • Demand for more efficient power electronics
  • 5G infrastructure deployment
  • Industrial automation trends

Segment Analysis

Key market segments include:

  • Automotive: Largest and fastest-growing segment, driven by EV powertrains
  • Energy: Solar inverters and wind turbine converters
  • Industrial: Motor drives and power supplies
  • Consumer: Fast chargers and premium appliances

Regional Outlook

Asia-Pacific leads in SiC adoption, followed by Europe and North America. China is the largest single market due to its dominance in EV manufacturing and renewable energy deployment.

Supply Chain Considerations

As demand grows, supply chain stability becomes increasingly important. Wolfspeed's capacity expansion and partnerships with distributors like LiTong Group help ensure reliable supply to customers worldwide.
