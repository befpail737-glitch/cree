Wolfspeed Increases SiC Wafer Production by 40%

February 28, 2024 By LiTong Group

Wolfspeed has announced a significant expansion of its Silicon Carbide (SiC) wafer fabrication capacity, increasing production by 40% to meet the growing demand from electric vehicle (EV) and renewable energy markets.

Expansion Details

The expansion includes new manufacturing equipment and facility upgrades at Wolfspeed's existing production sites. This investment will enable Wolfspeed to meet the rapidly increasing demand for SiC components used in power electronics applications.

Market Drivers

The growth in SiC demand is primarily driven by:

  • Electric vehicle powertrain applications
  • Renewable energy inverters
  • Industrial power supplies
  • Fast charging infrastructure

Impact on Supply Chain

This capacity expansion will help stabilize the SiC supply chain and reduce lead times for customers. As an authorized distributor, LiTong Group will benefit from improved product availability to serve our customers better.
