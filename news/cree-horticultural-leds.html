Cree Introduces Specialized Horticultural LED Series

January 22, 2024 By LiTong Group

Cree has introduced a new series of LEDs specifically optimized for horticultural applications. These LEDs feature enhanced red and blue wavelength output to maximize plant growth and yield.

Product Features

The new horticultural LED series includes:

  • Enhanced red (660nm) output for photosynthesis
  • Blue (450nm) wavelengths for vegetative growth
  • Far-red (730nm) options for photomorphogenesis
  • High efficacy to reduce energy costs
  • Long lifetime for reduced maintenance

Applications

These LEDs are ideal for:

  • Greenhouse supplemental lighting
  • Vertical farming systems
  • Research and development facilities
  • Commercial crop production

Technical Support

LiTong Group provides comprehensive technical support for horticultural LED applications, including spectral design assistance and thermal management guidance.
