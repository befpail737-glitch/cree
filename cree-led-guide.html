How to Choose the Right Cree LED

1. Determine Lumen Requirements

Calculate the total luminous flux needed for your application. Consider fixture efficiency and optical losses.

2. Select Power Class

  • Mid Power (J Series): General lighting, cost-sensitive
  • High Power (XLamp): High-output, outdoor, industrial
  • COB: Directional lighting, simple optics

3. Choose CCT & CRI

  • 2700K-3000K: Warm white, residential, hospitality
  • 4000K: Neutral white, commercial, office
  • 5000K-6500K: Cool white, industrial, outdoor
  • CRI 90+: Retail, museums, color-critical

4. Consider Thermal Design

Ensure adequate heat sinking to maintain LED junction temperature below maximum ratings for optimal life and performance.
