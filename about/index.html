About LiTong Group

Your trusted authorized distributor of Cree LED and Wolfspeed SiC products with over 18 years of industry experience and global reach.

Our Story

Founded in 2005, LiTong Group has established itself as a leading authorized distributor of Cree LED products and Wolfspeed Silicon Carbide (SiC) components. With over 18 years of experience in the electronics distribution industry, we have built strong partnerships with manufacturers and developed deep expertise in LED and power semiconductor technologies.

Our commitment to providing exceptional service and technical support has made us a trusted partner for engineers and procurement professionals worldwide. We maintain strong relationships with Cree, Inc. and Wolfspeed, ensuring access to the latest products and technical resources.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with the highest quality LED and SiC components, comprehensive technical support, and reliable global supply to enable innovation in lighting, power electronics, and other applications.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our business relationships
  • Technical Excellence: We provide deep technical expertise to support our customers' applications
  • Global Partnership: We serve customers across all markets with local support
  • Quality: We ensure genuine products and reliable services

Our Technical Expertise

LED Applications

Our team has extensive experience in LED lighting applications, including thermal management, optical design, and electrical design. We provide technical guidance for Cree XLamp, J series, and other LED products.

SiC Power Systems

We specialize in Silicon Carbide power applications, including gate drive design, thermal management, and system optimization. Our expertise covers Wolfspeed SiC MOSFETs and diodes.

Design Support

We offer comprehensive design support, from initial concept to production, helping customers implement Cree and Wolfspeed components in their applications.

Testing & Validation

Our facilities include optical, thermal, and electrical testing equipment to support customer validation processes.

Global Sales Network
