About Wolfspeed Power Modules

Wolfspeed power modules integrate high-performance SiC MOSFETs and diodes into compact, thermally optimized packages. These modules enable system designers to achieve higher power density, improved efficiency, and reduced cooling requirements compared to traditional silicon IGBT modules.

Key Advantages

  • Switching frequencies up to 100kHz (vs 10-20kHz for IGBTs)
  • 50% reduction in switching losses
  • Lower total system cost through reduced magnetics and cooling
  • Integrated temperature sensing and current sensing
  • Standard industrial packages (62mm, EasyPACK, etc.)

CAB Series - Wolfspeed Power Modules

High Performance

The CAB series power modules feature Wolfspeed's 3rd generation SiC MOSFET technology in industry-standard packages. Available in half-bridge, six-pack, and boost configurations to suit various application requirements.

Key Features

  • 1200V and 1700V voltage classes
  • Current ratings from 190A to 600A
  • Low inductance design for fast switching
  • Integrated NTC temperature sensor
  • AlSiC baseplate for optimal thermal performance

Half-Bridge

Six-Pack

  • Wind power
