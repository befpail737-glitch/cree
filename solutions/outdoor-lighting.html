Frequently Asked Questions

What are the advantages of LED over traditional outdoor lighting?

▼

LED outdoor lighting offers significant advantages over traditional technologies: up to 50% energy savings, longer lifespan (10+ years vs. 2-3 years for HPS/MH), better color rendering, instant-on capability, dimming compatibility, and reduced maintenance requirements. Additionally, LEDs provide better light control with precision optics, reducing light pollution.

How do Cree LEDs perform in harsh outdoor environments?

▼

Cree LEDs are designed for reliability in demanding applications. They feature robust package construction that withstands vibration, thermal cycling, and environmental stresses. When properly integrated into luminaires with appropriate thermal management and housing, Cree LED-based systems demonstrate exceptional longevity and lumen maintenance in outdoor environments.

What is the typical lifespan of Cree LED outdoor lighting?

▼

Cree LEDs are tested and rated to maintain 70% of initial light output (L70) for over 100,000 hours under appropriate operating conditions. Actual lifespan depends on thermal management, electrical design, and environmental factors. Our outdoor lighting solutions typically achieve 10-15 years of useful life in street lighting applications.
