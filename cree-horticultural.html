XP-E2 Photo Red

660nm Peak

The XP-E2 Photo Red delivers maximum photosynthetic photon flux at the 660nm wavelength, matching the peak absorption of chlorophyll A for optimal photosynthesis.

Key Specifications

  • Peak Wavelength: 660nm

XP-E2 Far Red

730nm Peak

The XP-E2 Far Red at 730nm controls the phytochrome photostationary state, influencing plant morphology, flowering, and shade avoidance responses.

Key Specifications

  • Peak Wavelength: 730nm
  • High radiant flux output
  • Package: 3.5mm x 3.5mm
  • Maximum Current: 1000mA

Applications

  • Photomorphogenic control
  • Flowering induction
  • Stem elongation control
  • End-of-day lighting
