Advantages of SiC Schottky Diodes

Zero recovery diodes for high efficiency

Zero Reverse Recovery

SiC Schottky diodes feature zero reverse recovery current, eliminating switching losses associated with diode recovery in traditional silicon diodes.

Applications

  • PFC circuits
  • DC-DC converters
  • Inverters
  • Rectifiers
