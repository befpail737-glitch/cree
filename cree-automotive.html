Electric Vehicle Power Electronics

Wolfspeed SiC power devices enable next-generation electric vehicle power systems with higher efficiency, smaller size, and improved thermal performance compared to traditional silicon solutions.

Applications

🔋 Traction Inverters

Main drive inverters for EV propulsion systems. SiC enables higher switching frequencies for improved motor efficiency and smaller filters.

🔌 On-Board Chargers (OBC)

High-efficiency battery charging systems. SiC reduces charging time and improves power density.
