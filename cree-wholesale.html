Wholesale FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ varies by product but typically starts at 1,000 units for standard LED products and 100 units for power devices. We can be flexible for initial evaluation orders.

Do you offer volume pricing?

Yes, we offer tiered volume pricing with discounts starting at 10K units and increasing significantly at higher volumes. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific requirements.

What payment terms do you offer for wholesale customers?

For qualified wholesale customers, we offer Net 30 terms. For larger volume commitments, we can discuss Net 60 or custom payment schedules. New customers typically start with prepayment or LC.

Can you support long-term supply agreements?

Yes, we offer annual and multi-year supply agreements with fixed pricing, dedicated inventory, and guaranteed allocation during supply constraints.

Do you provide technical support for large orders?

Yes, wholesale customers receive priority technical support including application engineering assistance, design reviews, and failure analysis when needed.
