Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from LiTong

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

We offer flexible MOQs to support both prototyping and production needs. Most standard products have MOQs of 100-500 pieces, but we can accommodate smaller quantities for samples and initial testing. For high-volume production, we offer volume pricing starting at 1,000+ pieces. Contact our sales team for specific MOQ information on your required parts.

How fast can you deliver to Germany and Europe?

For European customers, we offer 5-7 business day delivery via DHL Express or FedEx International Priority. All shipments include full customs documentation, CE compliance certificates, and commercial invoices. For urgent orders, we can arrange 3-day express shipping to major European cities. No import duties apply for intra-Asia shipments.

Do you offer free samples?

Yes, we provide free samples for qualified commercial projects. Sample requests are typically processed within 24 hours and shipped within 2-3 business days. We offer up to 5 free samples per part number for evaluation purposes. For larger sample quantities or specialized testing, please contact our applications engineering team.

How do I know your Cree products are genuine?

As an authorized Cree and Wolfspeed distributor since 2005, we provide full Certificates of Conformance (COC) with every order, complete traceability documentation back to the manufacturer, and full manufacturer warranty coverage. All products ship in original manufacturer packaging with authentic labels and serial numbers. We maintain direct relationships with Cree/Wolfspeed factories, eliminating the risk of counterfeits.

What is the difference between Cree and Wolfspeed?

Cree Inc. rebranded its Power and RF divisions as Wolfspeed in 2018. Today, Cree focuses exclusively on LED lighting products (XLamp, J Series), while Wolfspeed handles all power semiconductor products including SiC MOSFETs, SiC diodes, and RF components. As an authorized distributor for both brands, we supply the complete product portfolio under their respective brand names.

Do you provide design support?

Absolutely. Our team of 5 Field Application Engineers (FAEs) provides complimentary design support including schematic review, thermal analysis, PCB layout recommendations, and reference design consultation. We have over 200 proven reference designs available for LED lighting, EV charging, solar inverters, and motor drive applications. Support is available in English, Chinese, and German.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Wire Transfer (T/T), Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal for smaller orders, and Letter of Credit (L/C) for large transactions. Established customers may qualify for Net 30 payment terms after credit approval. All transactions are processed securely, and we provide proforma invoices for order confirmation.

Can you help with LED thermal management?

Yes, thermal management is critical for LED performance and longevity. Our FAE team provides thermal simulation support, heat sink selection guidance, and thermal interface material recommendations. We also offer detailed application notes on thermal design for Cree XLamp and J Series LEDs. For complex projects, we can review your thermal design and suggest optimizations.

Do you support custom LED binning?

Yes, we offer custom binning services for Cree LEDs to meet specific color temperature, flux, and voltage requirements. Custom binning is available for orders of 5,000+ pieces and can be arranged for both XLamp and J Series products. Lead time for custom binning is typically 4-6 weeks. Contact us with your specific binning requirements for a quotation.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for standard catalog items in original, unopened packaging. For defective products, we provide full replacement under manufacturer warranty (typically 5 years for LEDs, 2 years for power devices). Custom or special order items may have different return terms. All returns require prior authorization - please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Do you stock Wolfspeed SiC power modules?

Yes, we maintain significant inventory of Wolfspeed SiC power modules including the CAB450M12XM3, CAB650M17HM3, and other popular configurations. Our power modules are ideal for high-power applications such as EV charging stations, solar inverters, and industrial motor drives. All modules come with full manufacturer warranty and thermal interface materials. Contact us for specific module availability and pricing.

What Cree LED products are best for street lighting?

For street lighting applications, we recommend the Cree XLamp XP-L High Intensity and XHP70.3 LEDs, which deliver exceptional lumen output (up to 2,000+ lumens) with high efficacy (170+ lm/W). The J Series 2835 and 3030 LEDs are excellent for mid-power street light retrofits. Our FAE team can help you select the optimal LED based on your specific requirements for lumen output, color temperature (typically 3000K-5700K), and CRI (70-90). We also provide thermal management guidance for outdoor applications.

Can you support EV charging infrastructure projects?

Absolutely. We supply Wolfspeed SiC MOSFETs and power modules specifically designed for EV charging applications, from 7kW residential chargers to 350kW+ DC fast charging stations. Our C3M0016120K (1200V, 16mΩ) and C3M0060065K (650V, 6mΩ) SiC MOSFETs are widely used in EV charger designs for their high efficiency and fast switching capabilities. We provide reference designs, gate driver recommendations, and thermal management support for EV charging applications. Our local Asia stock ensures fast delivery for your production schedules.

Still have questions? Contact our team for personalized assistance.
