Why Choose Wolfspeed SiC Components

Higher Efficiency

SiC technology enables significantly higher efficiency in power conversion systems compared to traditional silicon components.

Higher Temperature Operation

SiC devices operate reliably at higher temperatures, reducing cooling requirements and system size.

System Size Reduction

Higher switching frequencies enabled by SiC allow for smaller magnetics and overall system miniaturization.
