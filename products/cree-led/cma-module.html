About the Cree CMA LED Module

The Cree CMA LED module is a high-flux Chip-on-Board (COB) solution that integrates multiple LED dies in a single package with an optimized thermal design. This advanced approach simplifies system design by reducing component count and assembly complexity while delivering exceptional luminous density and thermal performance. The CMA series represents Cree's latest advances in COB technology, offering superior performance compared to previous generations.

With its advanced integrated design, the CMA module offers even better thermal management than standard COB modules, as well as simplified optical design with reduced component count. The advanced ceramic substrate provides superior thermal conductivity and mechanical stability for reliable operation in the most demanding applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the CMA module differ from the CMB module?

The CMA module is Cree's advanced COB solution with higher flux density and improved thermal performance compared to the CMB series. The CMA series incorporates newer LED technology with enhanced efficacy and luminous density, making it suitable for applications requiring maximum light output in a compact form factor. It also features an improved thermal design for better heat dissipation.

What are the installation requirements for CMA modules?

CMA modules require careful attention to thermal management and electrical connections. The module must be mounted to a suitable heatsink using thermal interface material with low thermal resistance. Proper cleaning of surfaces and correct mounting pressure are critical for optimal thermal performance. Electrical connections must be made with appropriate wire gauge and connectors rated for the operating current.

Can CMA modules be used in outdoor applications?

Yes, CMA modules can be used in outdoor applications when properly integrated into a luminaire with appropriate environmental protection. The modules themselves are designed for harsh environments, but the overall system design must provide adequate protection against moisture, dust, and temperature extremes. Proper sealing and IP-rated enclosures are essential for reliable outdoor operation.
