About the Cree XLamp XP-G3 LED

The Cree XLamp XP-G3 LED represents the next generation in high-brightness LED technology. Building on the industry-leading performance of the original XP-G LED, the XP-G3 offers significant improvements in luminous efficacy, optical output, and reliability for general lighting applications.

This high-brightness LED is optimized for use in applications requiring high lumen density in a compact form factor. The XP-G3 series provides excellent color consistency and long-term reliability in a robust package suitable for a wide range of lighting applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between XP-G3 and the previous XP-G LED?

▼

The XP-G3 LED offers significant improvements over the original XP-G, including higher luminous efficacy, increased luminous flux, and improved reliability. The XP-G3 maintains pin compatibility with the XP-G, making it easy to upgrade existing designs.

What thermal management considerations should I keep in mind?

▼

For optimal performance and longevity, proper thermal management is critical. The LED should be mounted to a heatsink with good thermal conductivity. The junction temperature should be kept below the maximum rated value to ensure reliable operation and maintain lumen maintenance over time.

What are the color temperature options available?

▼

The XP-G3 is available in a wide range of color temperatures from 2700K to 10500K, allowing for flexibility in various lighting applications. The specific bins and availability depend on the flux and efficacy options selected.
