About the Cree XLamp XP-L LED

The Cree XLamp XP-L LED is designed for applications requiring high flux density in a compact, industry-standard package. This high-brightness LED delivers exceptional performance in a variety of lighting applications including general illumination, outdoor lighting, and specialized lighting applications. The XP-L series provides an optimal balance of flux density, efficacy, and reliability.

With its proven reliability and consistent performance, the XP-L series provides an excellent solution for applications where light output and longevity are important. The industry-standard package allows for design compatibility with existing systems while offering improved performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between XP-L and XP-E2 LEDs?

The XP-L LED offers higher flux density compared to the XP-E2, making it suitable for applications requiring maximum lumen output in a compact package. Both are part of Cree's XLamp series but are optimized for different performance requirements. The XP-L provides a balance of flux density and efficacy that makes it ideal for general lighting applications.

What thermal management considerations should I keep in mind?

For optimal performance and longevity, proper thermal management is critical. The LED should be mounted to a heatsink with good thermal conductivity. The junction temperature should be kept below the maximum rated value to ensure reliable operation and maintain lumen maintenance over time. Cree recommends following the thermal design guidelines provided in the product datasheet.

What are the color temperature options available?

The XP-L is available in a range of color temperatures from 2700K (warm white) to 8000K (cool white), allowing for flexibility in various lighting applications. The specific bins and availability depend on the flux and efficacy options selected.
