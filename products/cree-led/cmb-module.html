About the Cree CMB LED Module

The Cree CMB LED module is a high-flux Chip-on-Board (COB) solution that integrates multiple LED dies in a single package. This innovative approach simplifies system design by reducing component count and assembly complexity while delivering exceptional luminous density and thermal performance. The CMB series combines the proven reliability of Cree LED technology with the benefits of COB packaging.

With its integrated design, the CMB module offers improved thermal management compared to discrete LED arrays, as well as simplified optical design with reduced component count. The ceramic substrate provides excellent thermal conductivity and mechanical stability for reliable operation in demanding applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the advantage of COB modules over discrete LEDs?

COB modules like the CMB series offer several advantages: simplified optical design with fewer components, improved thermal management through the ceramic substrate, reduced assembly complexity, and higher luminous density in a compact form factor. The integrated design also reduces system-level costs by minimizing the number of individual components and assembly steps.

How should I mount a CMB module for optimal thermal performance?

For optimal thermal performance, the CMB module should be mounted to a heatsink using a high-quality thermal interface material (TIM) with low thermal resistance. The mounting surface should be flat and clean. Proper mounting pressure is important to ensure good thermal contact between the module and the heatsink. Refer to the mounting guide for specific torque values and procedures.

Can CMB modules be dimmed?

Yes, CMB modules can be dimmed using constant current drivers with PWM or analog dimming capabilities. Cree recommends following the dimming specifications in the product datasheet to ensure proper operation and longevity. Proper dimming implementation can extend module life and improve system efficiency.
