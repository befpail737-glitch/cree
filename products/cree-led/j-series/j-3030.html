About the Cree J Series J3030 LED

The Cree J Series J3030 LED is designed for applications where cost efficiency and ease of system design are important. This mid-power LED delivers excellent performance in a standard 3030 package, making it ideal for a wide range of lighting applications including residential, commercial, and industrial lighting.

With its proven reliability and consistent performance, the J3030 series provides an optimal balance of cost and performance. The compact package size allows for dense arrays while maintaining good thermal characteristics.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between J Series and XLamp Series LEDs?

▼

The J Series LEDs are optimized for applications where cost efficiency and ease of system design are important. They are available in standard 2835, 3030, and 5050 packages. The XLamp Series LEDs are designed for applications requiring maximum lumen density and highest efficacy, with more advanced thermal performance and higher power densities.

What thermal management considerations should I keep in mind?

▼

While J Series LEDs are designed for standard thermal management approaches, proper heat sinking is still critical for optimal performance and longevity. The 3030 package has good thermal characteristics, but the PCB design and mounting method should allow for effective heat dissipation. For continuous operation, keeping the junction temperature within recommended limits will ensure reliable operation and maintain lumen maintenance over time.

What are the color temperature options available?

▼

The J3030 is available in a range of color temperatures from 2700K (warm white) to 8000K (cool white), allowing for flexibility in various lighting applications. The specific bins and availability depend on the flux and efficacy options selected.
