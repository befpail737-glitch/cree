About the Cree J Series J5050 LED

The Cree J Series J5050 LED is designed for applications where cost efficiency and balanced performance are important. This mid-power LED delivers excellent performance in the industry-standard 5050 package, making it ideal for a wide range of lighting applications including residential, commercial, and industrial lighting. As a Cree LED agent, we provide genuine products with full technical support.

With its proven reliability and consistent performance, the J5050 series provides an optimal balance of cost and performance. The 5050 package offers higher flux capability than the 2835 and 3030 packages while maintaining cost effectiveness. This makes it suitable for applications requiring moderate to high flux output with good efficacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between J5050 and J3030 LEDs?

The J5050 LED comes in a larger 5050 package compared to the 3030 package of the J3030, allowing for higher flux output. The J5050 typically provides higher luminous flux and power handling capability, making it suitable for applications requiring more light output while still maintaining cost efficiency.

What thermal management considerations should I keep in mind?

For optimal performance and longevity, proper thermal management is critical. The LED should be mounted to a heatsink with good thermal conductivity. The junction temperature should be kept below the maximum rated value to ensure reliable operation and maintain lumen maintenance over time. Cree recommends following the thermal design guidelines provided in the product datasheet.

What are the color temperature options available?

The J5050 is available in a range of color temperatures from 2700K (warm white) to 8000K (cool white), allowing for flexibility in various lighting applications. The specific bins and availability depend on the flux and efficacy options selected.
