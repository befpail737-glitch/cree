XLamp XHP50 LED

High-intensity LED for extreme brightness

Product Overview

The XLamp XHP50 LED delivers extreme brightness with its high-density chip-on-board technology. It provides twice the light output of standard LEDs while maintaining excellent efficacy.

Key Features

  • Maximum drive current: 3000 mA
  • Up to 2546 lumens at 19W
  • High-density chip technology
  • 5mm x 5mm package
  • Excellent thermal performance

Applications

  • High-bay lighting
  • Street lighting
  • Spotlights
  • Stage lighting
  • High-performance flashlights
