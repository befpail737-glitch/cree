Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between SiC power modules and IGBT modules?

SiC power modules offer significantly lower switching losses (up to 80% reduction), higher switching frequencies (up to 100kHz vs 20kHz for IGBTs), and better thermal performance. This enables smaller system size, reduced cooling requirements, and higher efficiency (99% vs 97% for IGBTs).

Do you provide gate drivers for these power modules?

Yes, we can recommend compatible gate drivers for each power module. The CAB450M12XM3 and CAB650M17HM3 work with standard SiC MOSFET gate drivers with appropriate voltage ratings (+15V/-4V typical). We provide reference designs showing recommended gate driver circuits.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for power modules?

For standard catalog items like the CAB450M12XM3 and CAB650M17HM3, our MOQ is typically 10-50 pieces. For high-volume production, we offer volume pricing starting at 100+ pieces. Contact our sales team for specific pricing and availability.

Can you support custom power module configurations?

For high-volume applications (1,000+ units annually), Wolfspeed offers custom power module development. Our FAE team can discuss your specific requirements and connect you with Wolfspeed's application engineering team for custom solutions.
